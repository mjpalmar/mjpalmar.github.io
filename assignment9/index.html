Right now, I am an Assistant Sports Editor at The Daily Orange. I cover the women's basketball team for The D.O., and I also refine articles to ensure they met editorial standards, pitch headlines, craft tweets for articles and utilize WordPress to publish stories on the Daily Orange website. Before becoming an Assistant Sports Editor, I was an Assistant Digital Editor and a Staff Writer at The D.O. I have previously covered women's soccer, softball and field hockey for The D.O. In the photo to the left, I can be seen at Media Day for Syracuse women's basketball, interviewing head coach Felisha Legette-Jack.

I also managed the Anglers' social media presence on all platforms throughout the 2025 season.
